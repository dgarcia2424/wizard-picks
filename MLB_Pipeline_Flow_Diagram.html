Step 6 — Three-Part Lock Gate Detail

Lock 1 — Sanity
|P_model − P_pinnacle| ≤ 0.04
Rejects: model too far from market
Lock 2 — Odds Floor
American odds ≥ −225
Rejects: juice too high to overcome
Lock 3 — Edge (Tier 1)
EDGE_TIER1 = 0.030 (3.0%)
Full quarter-Kelly qualifying edge
Lock 3 — Edge (Tier 2)
EDGE_TIER2 = 0.010 (1.0%)
Half-stake eighth-Kelly opportunity
Pass →
Game advances to Kelly staking module
Reject →
Game logged to Supabase with rejection reason; not staked
