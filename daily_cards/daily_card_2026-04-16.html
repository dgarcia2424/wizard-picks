ⓘ GLOSSARY
PROBABILITIES
ModelOur simulation's estimated win/cover/over probability for this game
PinnacleSharp offshore book — de-vigged implied probability. Best market signal available. Used as the sanity check
RetailUS sportsbook (DraftKings / FanDuel / BetMGM) — de-vigged implied probability. This is what you actually bet into
EdgeModel prob minus Retail implied prob. Positive = model likes this side more than the market does
Band (3–7)25th–75th percentile range from the simulation. The game lands inside this range 50% of the time
BET MARKETS
MLMoneyline — straight win/loss bet. No spread. Model uses a 35% model / 65% Vegas blend since our sim only models pitching
RLRun Line — home team -1.5 spread. Home team must win by 2+ runs to cover. Primary model bet
O/UOver/Under — total combined runs scored. Model picks the better edge direction (OVER or UNDER)
K propStrikeout prop for the starting pitcher. Line = posted K total. P(O) = model's probability of going over that line. Edge = P(O) minus market implied
THREE-PART LOCK GATES
Gate 1: Sanity|Model − Pinnacle| must be ≤ 4%. If the model disagrees with the sharp market by more than 4 points, something is wrong — skip the game
Gate 2: Odds FloorRetail ML must be better than −225. Avoids heavy favorites where you risk too much to win too little
Gate 3: EdgeModel must beat the retail implied probability by ≥ 1.0%. This is the actual closing line value (CLV) edge you're betting on
TIER 1Edge ≥ 3.0% — strong signal. Quarter-Kelly sizing
TIER 2Edge ≥ 1.0% — medium signal. Eighth-Kelly sizing
